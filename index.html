GOALS, PLANS, & INITIATIVES

CHEAPER TEXTBOOKS (OER)

I am tired of paying ridiculous amounts of money for textbooks I will never use again. Open Educational Resources (or OER), are an initiative started by our current VP of Academics, that I intend to see through the finish line. What it technically means is low-cost or free open source textbook options for our general education classes. What it really means is less money spent on textbooks, which means more money for you to spend on literally anything else. You can read more about my plan here.

MENTAL HEALTH

Utah currently ranks 5 in the country for deaths by suicide, with 15% of those happening to 20-34 year olds. Thats not okay. There are several things we can do to combat this, and make sure our campus retains the lowest rate of suicide in the state. Through the introduction of the first ever collegiate Hope Squad, a lowering of Health and Wellness Center costs, and an expansion of the services offered on campus; we can work together to reduce suicide rates, increase overall mental health, and make our campus a safer, more positive place to live.

TRANSPARENCY

Nothing bums me out more than someone asking what Student Government even does. Student Government does an incredible amount of work for the student association (aka, every student at DSU). Its more than planning parties, its initiatives, bills, club organization, student representation, working with faculty and administration; and tons more. An increase in transparency and awareness will not only inform students, but make them more aware of what opportunities are present for them to take advantage of. Do you have questions about what we do? Hit me up.
